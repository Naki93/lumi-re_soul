DIGITAL — 2026

Aura Studio

A calming digital presence crafted for a wellness and lifestyle brand

THE PROBLEM

Aura Studio had built a devoted following through their wellness offerings but their digital presence told a different story. Their website felt cluttered and anxious — the opposite of everything their brand promised. They needed a digital home that felt as restorative as walking through their door.

THE APPROACH

Lumière Soul stripped everything back. We designed a minimal, breathable digital experience built on generous whitespace, soft tonal photography and a gentle typographic hierarchy. Every decision was filtered through one question — does this create calm or chaos?

THE OUTCOME

Aura Studio launched to an immediate response from their community. The new digital presence reflected their values with precision — a fully responsive website, refined content strategy and a visual system that finally matched the stillness they had always stood for.
